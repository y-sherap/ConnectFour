Connect Four

Game Instructions

Objective

Be the first player to connect 4 of the same colored discs either horizontally, veritcally, or diagonally

How To Play

  1. Decide who plays first. That player will be assigned a red disc.
  2. Player 2 will be assigned a yellow disc.
  3. Player 1 will start the game by clicking on one of the empty white cells on the bottom row.
  4. With the exception of the empty cells on the bottom row, players will only be able to place their disc the cell below it has already been played.
  5. Players will continue alternating turns until the game ends.
  6. The game ends when there are four of the same colored discs in a row or if there is a draw.
  7. Players can click on the "Restart" button to play again or reset the board at any time.
